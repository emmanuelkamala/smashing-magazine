SmashingConf Toronto 2019

Improving accessibility and front-end workflow, refactoring, security, interface design patterns, CSS Grid techniques, performance, Git and how to write better JavaScript.

With a strong background in human interface and user-centered design, Tantek Çelik has been advocating for open web standards for over 20 years. He is Web Standards Lead at Mozilla, founder of and community leader at Indie Webcamp and Microformats, and a member of the W3C CSS Working Group where he is one of the principal editors of several CSS specifications. Tantek wrote HTML5 Now and shares his thoughts and news on tantek.com. You can find him as @t on Twitter. Thank you for helping shape the web, dear Tantek!
